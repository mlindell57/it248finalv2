Project Priorities Overview

A summary of security, systems design, user experience, and information architecture considerations.

Security and Ethics in Information Architecture

Security and Ethics in IA is the most important because it not only protects your customers from bad actors, but it protects your reputation as a business providing a service. An article from NSG states “When customers interact with a business, they entrust their personal information, assets, and well-being.” This highlights the importance of security to keep your customers coming back; if they feel like their security is not at risk, they will be more likely to return to your website.

Systems Design, Engineering, and Organization

Systems Design is ranked second because the way a system is designed not only affects the user’s mood, but it is also important that the design can be scalable as the size of your organization grows.

User Experience and Visual Design

Having an appealing design works hand-in-hand with good digital-environment design. It improves the experience for users and entices them to utilize the environments that we are creating.

Content and Information Architecture

Efficient Information Architecture allows information to flow smoothly through the entire environment. If you have different ways to access environments, a solid Information Architecture will make transitions fluid. A well-made IA will encourage users to discover products they might not have initially sought.
